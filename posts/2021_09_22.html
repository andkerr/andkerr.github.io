What are my math skills? I hope writing some of these down will let me take some ownership of what I know, and create a feeling of "having a toolset" at my disposal when solving problems. This list will make little mention of classes of problems to which these techniques are applied (e.g. solutions to a Simple Harmonic Oscillator).

  • Calculus
    • Differentiation and Integration
    • Evaluating Common Taylor Series
    • Evaluating Limits (incl. Multivariable Functions)
    • Lagrange Multipliers
    • Vector and Scalar Fields
    • Calculating Gradient, Divergence, Curl
    • Evaluating Line Integrals
    • Applying Green's Theorem, Stoke's Theorem
    • Space Curves

  • Trigonometry
    • Similar Triangles
    • Laws of Sines and Cosines

  • Complex Numbers

  • Linear Algebra

  • Differential Equations

  • Fourier Analysis
